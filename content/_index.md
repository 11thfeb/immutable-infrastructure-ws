# Packer/Ansible/Terraform for Immutable Infrastructure

### Overall
In this workshop, we'll explore the fundamentals and practical exercises of Packer, Ansible, and Terraform for Immutable Infrastructure. These tools are essential for automating your infrastructure provisioning and deployment processes. You'll gain hands-on experience by creating connections for both public and private instances, enabling you to streamline your development workflow effectively. Let's dive in and discover the capabilities of Packer, Ansible, and Terraform together
